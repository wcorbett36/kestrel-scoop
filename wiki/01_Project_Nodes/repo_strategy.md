# repo_strategy

## Summary
The 'repo_strategy' project outlines the domains and purpose of the repositories within the local-first AI laboratory, focusing on Compute & Execution, Trust & Governance, and Publishing & Distribution. It describes the technical interfaces and how these components interact. The roadmap outlines strategic efforts to finalize the execution, governance, and publishing layers of the Artemis repository, focusing on completing tactical gaps in each domain without introducing new initiatives.

## Strategic Gap Analysis
The project faces strategic gaps in the completion of tactical gaps within the Compute & Execution, Trust & Governance, and Publishing domains. There is a need to ensure that the technical interfaces are well-defined and that the components interact seamlessly. Additionally, there is a need to ensure that the observability parity is maintained across all layers of the repository.
